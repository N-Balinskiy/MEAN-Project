Rules on MEAN

  • Be respectful: Do not post anything that is offensive, discriminatory, or defamatory towards other users. Respect other users' opinions and beliefs, even if they differ from your own.
  • No spam: Do not post spammy or promotional content. This includes excessive self-promotion, links to unrelated websites, or any other content that is not relevant to the website's purpose.
  • Original content: Only post original content that you have the right to publish. Do not plagiarize or copy content from other websites or users without their permission.
  • Image guidelines: Only post images that you have the right to publish. Make sure that the images are appropriate for all audiences and do not contain offensive or explicit content.
  • Comment guidelines: When commenting on other users' posts, be constructive and add value to the conversation. Do not post spammy or promotional comments, or engage in personal attacks or harassment.
  • Report violations: If you come across any content or behavior that violates these rules, report it to the website's admin immediately. Do not engage in arguments or fights with other users.
  • Consequences for rule-breaking: Violating any of these rules may result in a warning, suspension, or permanent ban from the website. If you are banned and have questions or concerns, you can contact the website's admin at mean-admin@gmail.com.

Remember, the purpose of these rules is to create a safe and respectful community for all users. By following these guidelines, you can help maintain a positive environment and enjoy your experience on the website.
